Stay Active, Stay Healthy

Exercise is not just about losing weight — it’s about improving your quality of life, boosting your mood, and keeping your body strong and flexible. Here are some recommended exercises to keep you fit and energized.

Yoga

Improves flexibility, reduces stress, and enhances mental focus. Try 20–30 minutes of yoga daily.

Jogging

A great cardio workout to strengthen your heart and lungs. Aim for 3–4 times per week.

Strength Training

Builds muscle, boosts metabolism, and improves bone health. Include 2–3 sessions weekly.

Cycling

Excellent for building leg strength and endurance while being gentle on your joints.

Tips for Better Sleep

  • Maintain a consistent sleep schedule, even on weekends.
  • Create a relaxing bedtime routine to signal your body it's time to wind down.
  • Limit exposure to screens at least 1 hour before bedtime.
  • Keep your sleeping environment cool, dark, and quiet.

Effective Sleep Routines

  • Try relaxing activities like reading, meditation, or gentle stretching before bed.
  • Avoid heavy meals, caffeine, and alcohol close to bedtime.
  • Use your bed only for sleep and intimacy to strengthen mental associations.
  • Go to bed only when you feel sleepy to avoid frustration.

Finding Your Perfect Sleep Time

The optimal amount of sleep varies by individual, but adults typically need 7-9 hours each night. Here are some tips to find your perfect sleep time:

  • Track your sleep patterns with a journal or sleep app for 1-2 weeks.
  • Note how you feel during the day relative to hours slept.
  • Adjust your bedtime and wake time in 15-30 minute increments based on how rested you feel.
  • Try to wake up naturally without an alarm to identify your natural sleep cycle.

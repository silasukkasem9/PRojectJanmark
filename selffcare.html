Daily Self-Care Routines

  • Start your day with a mindful meditation or breathing exercise.
  • Take regular breaks during work to stretch and hydrate.
  • Spend at least 30 minutes outdoors to boost mood and vitamin D.
  • Practice journaling to process thoughts and feelings.
“How you start your day often sets the tone for the rest of it.”

Mental Health Tips

  • Set realistic expectations and celebrate small victories.
  • Connect regularly with friends and family for support.
  • Limit screen time and social media consumption.
  • Seek professional help when feeling overwhelmed.

Relaxation Techniques

  • Try progressive muscle relaxation or guided imagery.
  • Engage in hobbies that bring joy and relaxation.
  • Use aromatherapy with essential oils like lavender or eucalyptus.
  • Practice gentle yoga or stretching before bed.

Building Healthy Boundaries

  • Learn to say “no” without guilt when you need rest.
  • Protect your personal time by setting clear limits at work or school.
  • Surround yourself with people who respect your needs.
  • Balance giving to others with giving to yourself.
